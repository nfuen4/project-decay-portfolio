## 6 Identified Gaps (Prioritized by Impact)

### 🔴 Gap 1: No AI Risk Management Framework (Highest Priority)
**What's missing:** There is no structured way to identify, score, and mitigate AI-specific project risks.
**PMI adds:** AI risk taxonomies — hallucination risk, model drift, data privacy exposure, over-reliance on AI output. Includes a risk register template with likelihood/impact scoring.
**Where it fits:** New sub-section in ⚙️ Project Scaffolding + new column in the Blank Canvas Risk Register.

---

### 🔴 Gap 2: No AI Ethics & Governance Gate
**What's missing:** The QA rubric checks brand/design/tone — but there's no compliance or ethics checkpoint before publishing AI outputs.
**PMI adds:** A structured "Responsible AI Checklist" that validates outputs for bias, PHI/PII exposure, and appropriate human oversight before delivery.
**Where it fits:** New tab or sub-section: `🛡️ AI Governance & Ethics`

---

### 🟠 Gap 3: No Token Budget & Cost Management Protocol
**What's missing:** Token limits are mentioned informally (you've struggled with this), but there's no systematic framework for estimating, allocating, and tracking token usage per project phase.
**PMI adds:** Resource management principles translated to AI compute costs. Treat tokens like sprint points — estimate before starting, track burn rate, trigger alerts when approaching limits.
**Where it fits:** New section in ⚙️ Project Scaffolding as Step 4.

---

### 🟠 Gap 4: No Value Realization / Outcome Measurement System
**What's missing:** Projects are scoped and built, but there's no standard way to measure whether the AI-orchestrated output actually achieved the stated business goal.
**PMI adds:** Benefits Realization Management (BRM) adapted for AI — define success metrics upfront, run a post-delivery review, track whether the target metric moved.
**Where it fits:** New section in 🗺️ Playbook & Mindset + new template section in Blank Canvas.

---

### 🟡 Gap 5: No Structured Retrospective Protocol
**What's missing:** After each project or sprint, there's no structured retrospective to extract what worked vs. what failed in the AI orchestration pipeline.
**PMI adds:** AI-adapted "Lessons Learned" format — specifically capturing: which prompts were high-yield, which subagent configurations failed, what caused context blowouts, and what permission patterns are reusable.
**Where it fits:** New section at the bottom of Blank Canvas (post-delivery section).

---

### 🟡 Gap 6: No AI Model Selection Criteria
**What's missing:** No framework for deciding when to use a lightweight model vs. a heavy flagship model — leading to unnecessary token burn on simple tasks.
**PMI adds:** Resource optimization logic — a simple decision tree for model tier selection based on task complexity, context requirements, and cost sensitivity.
**Where it fits:** New sub-section in 🚀 AI Agent Delegation tab.
